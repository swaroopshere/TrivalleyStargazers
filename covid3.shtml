Staying Healthy Rules

Due to the COVID-19 health crisis and because we're all more aware of disease spread, we ask that you consider the following for observing sites at Del Valle and H2O:
  • You use the observing sites at your own risk and agree to hold the club and the landowners free of all liability
  • Please consider others when choosing to participate in group activities, if you're ill, avoid spreading it
  • Announce your intention to use either site on the club's groups.io group, this helps in coordination
  • H2O users that wish to use the Quick Dome should first contact Ross Gaunt to reserve it
